About me

Hi, I'm Muhammed Sheheem, a self-taught full stack web developer from Malappuram, Kerala. I started my journey in web development after completing my +2 in science and then pursuing a degree that I ultimately decided to drop.

While I was studying, I worked as a union member and also volunteered with the National Service Scheme. These experiences taught me the value of hard work, collaboration, and community involvement.

Since then, I've been passionate about web development and have been working hard to improve my skills. I taught myself full stack web development and have created several projects on my own that I'm really proud of.

I specialize in MERN stack development and am confident in my ability to take on any project. My goal is to create high-quality, user-friendly web applications that solve real-world problems.

When I'm not coding, you can find me reading books, playing sports, or spending time with my family and friends.

If you have a project in mind or just want to say hello, don't hesitate to get in touch. I'd love to hear from you!
